Characteristic Qualities

Water Proof

PVC / WPC boards are 100% water proof and can be used in kitchen and other wet areas.

Fire Retardant

PVC / WPC boards are fire retardant and restricts the spread of fire.

Termite Proof

PVC / WPC boards are termite proof and protects your furniture from all types of pests.

Corrosion Proof

ERGO PVC & WPC Board is chemical resistant and corrosion proof. It is immune to the damage caused by oxidation and all other natural chemical reactions.

Eco Friendly

PVC / WPC boards are enivironmental friendly as no trees or forests are cut to produce them. PVC / WPC boards are recyclable.

Easy to Install

PVC / WPC boards are carpenter friendly as they are light weight, maintenance free and easy to install. PVC / WPC boards are also strong, durable and long lasting.
